## Razorpay Onboarding Knowledge Base:

### Stage 2 - Business Profile:
- Business types: Proprietorship, Partnership, LLP, Pvt Ltd, Public Ltd, Trust, Society, NGO
- Proprietorship is the simplest - just needs PAN and business proof
- Business category determines what products/payment methods are available
- Registered address should match official documents

### Stage 3 - KYC / Stakeholder Verification:
- Mandatory: PAN Card (individual or business)
- For Proprietors: Aadhaar + one of (GST Certificate, Shop Act License, FSSAI License, etc.)
- For Companies: Certificate of Incorporation, Board Resolution, MOA/AOA
- GST is NOT mandatory for all business types
- Documents are encrypted with bank-grade 256-bit encryption
- Razorpay is PCI DSS Level 1 certified and RBI regulated
- Verification usually takes 1-2 business days

### Stage 4 - Bank Account:
- Proprietors CAN use savings accounts
- Companies/LLPs MUST use current accounts
- Bank name must match the business name on KYC documents
- Penny drop verification: Razorpay deposits Re 1 to verify the account
- If verification fails: check IFSC code, account number, and name match

### Stage 5 - Website / App Details:
- Must have: Live website OR app on Play Store/App Store
- Mandatory pages: Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, Refund/Cancellation Policy, Contact Us
- Razorpay provides policy page templates
- No website? Use Payment Links (share on WhatsApp) or Payment Pages (hosted checkout)
- Social media sellers can submit their Instagram/Facebook shop URL

### Stage 6 - Integration:
- No-code options: Payment Links, Payment Buttons, Payment Pages
- Plugins available for: Shopify, WooCommerce, Wix, Magento, WordPress, OpenCart
- API integration for custom builds (requires developer)
- Test mode available with test API keys (no real money)
- Documentation: https://razorpay.com/docs/
- SDKs: JavaScript, PHP, Python, Ruby, Java, .NET, Go

### Stage 7 - Go Live:
- Final review by Razorpay compliance team
- Usually takes 2-3 business days
- May request additional documents
- Common rejection reasons: Prohibited business type, missing policy pages, KYC mismatch
- After activation: Switch from test to live API keys
